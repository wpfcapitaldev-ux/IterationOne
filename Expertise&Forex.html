Asset Class Knowledge

every investable asset class.

Equity remains the primary wealth-creation asset class for long-term investors. Our equity advisory spans direct equity strategies (through PMS), equity mutual funds across market caps, and tactical sector/thematic positioning.

We evaluate fund managers on rolling returns, downside protection, portfolio concentration, and style consistency — not just point-to-point NAV performance.

Our equity philosophy: quality compounders at the core, tactical tilts at the margin, and absolute discipline in not chasing recent performance.

Fixed income is not just a parking ground for short-term money. A well-constructed debt portfolio can meaningfully contribute to total portfolio returns — especially in a rate easing cycle.

We navigate the full fixed income spectrum: liquid funds for emergency reserves, short-duration for 1–3 year horizons, long-duration for rate cycle plays, and select NCDs and bonds for yield enhancement.

Credit quality discipline is non-negotiable. We do not chase yield through credit risk that isn't adequately compensated.

Current 10-yr G-Sec yield range6.8–7.2%
Minimum credit rating (non-liquid)AA–
Preferred duration in easing cycle5–7 Yrs
Liquid fund yield (indicative)7.2%

Alternative investments — PMS, AIF Category I/II/III, structured products — are appropriate for investors with ₹50L+ in investable assets and a clear strategic rationale for the allocation.

We are empanelled with select Category II and III AIF managers and evaluate them on: strategy differentiation from mutual funds, team pedigree, fee structure, liquidity terms, and verified track record over a full market cycle.

We do not recommend alternatives to fill a product allocation quota. Every alternative recommendation has a specific portfolio role that cannot be filled by a simpler, cheaper product.

Minimum investable assets for AIF₹5 Cr+
Minimum AIF investment (SEBI)₹1 Crore
PMS minimum₹50 Lakhs
AIF managers empanelled5+

Insurance is not an investment — it is a risk transfer mechanism, and the two should never be confused. Our insurance advisory starts with this principle and works outward: calculate your protection need, choose the most cost-efficient product, and never pay for coverage you don't need.

We are empanelled with Tata AIG, Cigna, Liberty Mutual, Birla Sun Life, LIC, and others — giving us the ability to genuinely compare and recommend across the market.

We conduct a full insurance audit for every new client — mapping existing covers, identifying gaps and redundancies, and presenting a protection architecture that is proportional to their risk profile.

Recommended term cover (vs income)15–20x
Recommended health cover (metro family)₹20–25L

Real assets — primarily residential and commercial real estate — form a meaningful part of most HNI balance sheets in India. We don't advise on property transactions directly, but we do advise on the financial optimisation of real estate holdings: mortgage structuring, Loan Against Property strategy, and the role of real estate in portfolio construction.

We help clients understand when their real estate is working hard for them — and when it is simply the largest illiquid asset on their balance sheet, not contributing to either yield or growth.
